Youth in Tune

Connecting young musicians with their community, one event at a time.

Our Story

We're Antone and Nathaniel, two 16-year-old musicians from San Mateo who got tired of seeing talented young artists with nowhere to perform. So we started Youth in Tune to change that.

We connect young musicians in our area with people who need live music for their events – birthdays, small gatherings, community events, you name it. The musicians get real performance experience, and you get amazing live music that won't break the bank.

It's pretty simple: young talent deserves a chance to shine, and every event deserves that special touch that only live music can bring. We're just here to make those connections happen.

How It Works

We work with young musicians in the San Mateo area who are serious about their craft and excited to perform. They volunteer their time to play at events, gaining valuable experience while bringing joy to the community.

Whether you need a guitarist for your backyard party, a pianist for your anniversary dinner, or a full band for your neighborhood event, we've got talented young musicians ready to make your occasion special.
